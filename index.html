Products

Arabica Products are designed to resolve the challenges of enterprise transformational change. Use them individually or combine them together to create the best solution for you.

HYPERVISORS AND TECHNOLOGY
With a focus on Xen and VMWare we can also cover a hypervisor type of your choice. We will tailor our solution to your existing technology stack.
DIRECTORY SERVICES AUTOMATION
Automate the provisioning of users, groups, permissions and policies.
RESOURCE SCANNER
Scan your entire virtualisation investment with a hypervisor agnostic service that perpetually collects metrics such as RAM, CPU and storage utilisation. Seamlessly introduce new capacity, automatically open and close resource pools and more.
TIN SCANNER
Gain visibility from the ground up, allowing you to understand the hardware on which your estate runs. In one location understand the OS, version, patch ref, availability, used storage, last use and power status of all your machines.
INTELLIGENT RESOURCE PLACEMENT
Allocate compute resource based on strategies that matter to you. Favour redundancy? deploy a particular service across many nodes. Favour cost savings? deploy to the cheapest hardware. Create your own custom policies that can be applied globally or to specific environments or platforms. Combine with the Resource Scanner for placement.
UNDERSTAND THE COSTS
Apply costs to your On-Prem infrastructure and track those of your Cloud investments. Gain reporting at a high and low level to understand cost per team, service, hypervisor and more. Discover unused and under utilised resources and put them to better use. Combine with the Resource Scanner / Tin Scanner to see where Teams are over provisioning resource and tighten up your Deploy/Destroy cycle.

TEMPLATING/IMMUTABLE BUILDS

Use templating to get build consistency across your estate, and utilise tools like chef to gain immutability.
